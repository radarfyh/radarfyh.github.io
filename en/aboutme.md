We are a software technology team focused on enterprise digitalization and intelligent solutions.  
Our mission is to help organizations transition from basic information systems to AI-driven operations through stable, efficient, and scalable technologies.

---

### What We Do

We focus on core business scenarios and provide three key capabilities:

- **Enterprise System Development**  
  Custom CRM, OA, and ERP systems with private deployment and flexible customization  

- **AI Agent Solutions**  
  LLM-based intelligent agents deeply integrated into business workflows  

- **Intelligent Workflow & Data Systems**  
  Connecting processes, data, and decision-making to enable automation and intelligence  

We deliver not just software, but full lifecycle support—from requirement analysis and development to deployment and continuous optimization.

### Our Journey

Our evolution reflects how we continuously adapt to enterprise needs:

- **2013**  
  Company founded, started with website development services  

- **2015**  
  Launched industry platform products, building domain experience  

- **2016**  
  Entered OA system integration, expanding into enterprise management systems  

- **2019**  
  Released industry tools, strengthening application-layer capabilities  

- **2020**  
  Expanded into CRM systems, enhancing customer management solutions  

- **2022**  
  Transitioned into AI-driven solutions, focusing on intelligent systems and learning platforms  

> From websites → enterprise systems → AI platforms  
> Our focus remains the same:  
> **improving how organizations acquire information, manage processes, and make decisions.**
