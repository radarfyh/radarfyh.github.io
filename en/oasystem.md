The OA system is designed for **teams with workflow management needs and enterprises requiring cross-team collaboration**. It addresses internal process confusion, delayed approvals, unclear task ownership, lack of traceability, and low communication efficiency. By providing workflow automation, task collaboration, and intelligent analytics, the system enables efficient management and transparent collaboration.

---

## I. Business Value of OA

- **Transparent Team Collaboration**: All team and role operations are traceable, and task status is visible in real time  
- **Business Process Automation**: Automate approvals, projects, expenses, procurement, and more  
- **Trackable Approval Chain**: Complete end-to-end recording from initiation to completion  
- **Reduced Internal Communication Costs**: Fast communication via notifications, task reminders, and collaboration comments  

---

## II. Core Features

- **Workflow Management**  
  - Approval processes: contract approval, procurement requests, expense reimbursements  
  - Project workflows: task assignment, project progress tracking  
  - Custom workflows: low-code configuration to meet specific enterprise requirements

- **Schedule and Task Management**  
  - Create, view, and edit schedules  
  - Daily, weekly, and monthly views  
  - Task assignment and reminders

- **Notifications and Announcements**  
  - Publish announcements and send messages  
  - Support targeting departments, individuals, or the entire organization  

- **Document Collaboration**  
  - File upload, sharing, and version control  
  - Multi-user collaboration and commenting  

- **Attendance Management**  
  - Clock-in, leave, shift adjustment, overtime tracking  
  - Integration with payroll and performance evaluation  

- **Permission Management**  
  - Role-based permissions  
  - Menu, feature, and data access control  
  - Audit logs and operation records

## IV. Differentiation Highlights

- **AI Smart Approval Suggestions**: System automatically recommends approval decisions based on historical data and rules  
- **AI Automatic Weekly/Monthly Report Summaries**: Save manual statistics time and improve decision-making efficiency
